Mloukhieh

Description

Syrian mloukhia is a savory, green stew made from chopped jute leaves that are simmered in a flavorful broth, often with garlic, coriander, and meat like lamb or chicken. It has a unique, slightly slimy or silky texture and is typically served with white rice and lemon wedges
