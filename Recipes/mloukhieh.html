Mloukhieh

Description

Syrian mloukhia is a savory, green stew made from chopped jute leaves that are simmered in a flavorful broth, often with garlic, coriander, and meat like lamb or chicken. It has a unique, slightly slimy or silky texture and is typically served with white rice and lemon wedges

Ingredients :

  • Chicken Prep
    • 2 Large Chicken Breasts roughly 1.25 lbs. You can use bone-in chicken or dark meat as well.
    • salt & Pepper to taste
    • 0.5 tbsp Garlic Powder
    • 0.5 tbsp Paprika
    • 0.5 tbsp Ground Coriander
    • 1 Small Onion, quartered
    • 2 Bay Leaves
    • 4 Garlic cloves
  • Mloukhieh Prep
    • 2 14 oz Frozen Mlokhia Bags
    • 1.5 cups Chicken Broth *may need more
    • â–¢ 1 tbsp Olive oil
    • 4 Garlic cloves, minced
  • Toppings
    • half a Jalapeno
    • 1 Small Lemon's juice
    • 2 Garlic cloves
    • 2 tbsp Olive oil
    • pinch of salt

Steps :

  1. First, work on the preperation of the chicken. Add the chicken breast to a pot and season it with salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika and ground coriander.
  2. Cover the chicken enitely with warm water. Add the onion, bay leaves, and garlic cloves. Let this come to a boil then bring to a simmer for about 20-25 minutes until fully cooked through.
  3. Once chicken is cooked, take it out and let it cool for a few minutes then shred with your hands or a fork.
  4. Strain the broth and set aside to use.
  5. Add the frozen mlokhia to a pot and add 1.5 cups of broth to this. Season with a bit of salt. Allow to simmer for 10 minutes. The minced mlokhia will melt into the stock creating the stew.
  6. Meanwhile, make the salsa. In a mini food processor, blend the jalapeno, lemon juice, garlic, pinch of salt and olive oil. Adjust to taste.
  7. Saute the freshly minced garlic cloves in the olive oil until fragrant and crisp. Add this to the mlokhia.
  8. Add in the shredded chicken as well; warm together for a minutes then serve on a bed of rice with the salsa and lemon wedges.
