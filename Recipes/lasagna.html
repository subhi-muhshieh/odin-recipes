Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish of wide, flat pasta layered with fillings like ragù (meat and tomato sauce), béchamel sauce, vegetables, and cheese (such as ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan).

After being assembled, it is baked in an oven until golden and bubbly. Lasagna can also refer simply to the large, flat noodle itself.

Ingredients :

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1.5 teaspoons dried basil
  • 0.5 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for sautéing
  • 16 ounces whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 0.5 cup grated Parmesan cheese, plus more for topping
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, plus more for garnish
  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps :

  1. Cook and drain the ground meat and onions.
  2. Simmer the meat with tomato sauce, herbs, and seasonings.
  3. While the sauce simmers, boil the lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and rinse them.
  4. Combine ricotta cheese, eggs, and seasonings for a ricotta mixture.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of the baking dish.
  6. Arrange a single layer of noodles over the sauce.
  7. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  8. Add a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  9. Spoon more meat sauce over the cheese.
  10. Repeat the layers: noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and sauce.
  11. Finish with a final layer of noodles and spread any remaining sauce on top.
  12. Sprinkle the top with a final layer of shredded cheese, such as Parmesan.
  13. Preheat the oven to 190 Degrees celsius (375 F).
  14. Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil, ensuring it does not touch the cheese.
  15. Bake for 45 minutes.
  16. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  17. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving
